ASON JESSE X SANTA CRUZ X CONS

In celebration of 40 years of Santa Cruz Skateboards, Converse has collaborated on some limited edition skate sneakers. Long time Santa Cruz Pro and Converse Ambassador Jason Jessee provided the inspiration for the Converse CONS CTS pack in both Mid and Ox. To celebrate the sneaker collaboration we helped Jason build a classic vert ramp near his home in Santa Cruz. The video shows Jason helping to build the ramp and then proceeding to shred it in classic Jason fashion.

BRIXTON X KROOKED PRESENT THE MISSION JACKET

Brixton and Krooked have teamed up and present the Mission jacket, a limited edition diamond quilted nylon jacket featuring custom Brixton and Krooked artwork, contrast color lining, ribbed cuffs and an inside chest pocket.
